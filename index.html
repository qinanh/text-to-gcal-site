Text to Google Calendar

Turn any text into a Google Calendar event with AI. Select text on any webpage and instantly create perfectly formatted calendar events.

What it does

Text to Google Calendar is a Chrome extension that uses AI to extract event details from any text you select and create them as Google Calendar events. No more manual data entry.

Features

  • Right-click any selected text to instantly create calendar events
  • AI-powered parsing of dates, times, locations, and attendees
  • Supports multiple events from a single block of text
  • Preview events before creating them
  • Recurring events, reminders, Google Meet links, and more

Privacy

Your privacy matters. The extension only accesses your Google Calendar when you explicitly trigger an action. Authentication is handled directly in your browser, and your Google credentials are never sent to or stored on our servers.
